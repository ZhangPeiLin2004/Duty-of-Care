How to Use Alternative Toolbox

Overview

The PFAS Toolbox helps you explore per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS). You can view their core functions, elemental composition, recommended alternatives, changes in functionality, market uptake, and hazard classifications.

Search & Filter

  • Search by Name or CAS: Type a substance name or CAS number into the search bar for instant filtering.
  • Use Category / Sub-Use / Applications: Multi-select filters narrow results; click “×” on tags to remove.
  • Clear All Filters: Click “Clear All Filters” to reset.

List View

Filtered results appear as cards showing the substance’s name, CAS, and elemental composition. Click “View Details” on a card to see full information.

Detail View

  • Basic Info: Name, CAS, use category, sub-use, applications, elemental composition.
  • Functions: Technical function, end-use function, service, and performance requirements.
  • Alternatives: Recommended alternative substance and type.
  • Change in Functionality: Notes on how functionality may change.
  • Market Uptake: Adoption status of the alternative.
  • PBT & CLP: Persistence, bioaccumulation, toxicity assessment, and hazard classification.

Data Sources

All data is sourced from the ZeroPM Alternative Assessment database. Raw JSON files are stored in the data/ folder for your reference.

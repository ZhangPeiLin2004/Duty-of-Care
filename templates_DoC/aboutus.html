About Us

We are a team of Computational Social Science students passionate about using data and digital tools to drive social impact. Our current focus is on supporting the implementation of the Duty of Care for PFAS (per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances) in the Netherlands. Combining research, focus group analysis, and community engagement, we aim to make the complexities of PFAS more accessible to researchers, companies and decision-makers. Through our work, we seek to promote accountability, raise awareness, and contribute to a more transparent and responsible approach to environmental health and environmental justice.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance the implementation of the Duty of Care for PFAS in the Netherlands by combining community engagement and policy advocacy. We strive to bridge the gap between scientific research and practical solutions, empowering stakeholders—from policymakers to citizens—to make informed decisions about environmental health risks associated with PFAS contamination. Through transparency and innovative digital tools, we aim to foster accountability and contribute to a safer, more sustainable environment for current and future generations.
